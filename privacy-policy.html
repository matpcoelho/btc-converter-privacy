Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 26, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how the Bitcoin Price Converter Chrome extension ("we", "our", or "the extension") collects, uses, and protects your information.

2. Information Collection

The extension collects and processes the following information:

  • Price information from web pages you visit (when the extension is enabled)
  • User preferences (display settings and conversion preferences)
  • Price tracking history (when explicitly saved by user)

3. Use of Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Converting prices to Bitcoin equivalents
  • Storing your preferences
  • Maintaining price tracking history
  • Providing analytics on tracked prices

4. Third-Party Services

The extension uses the CoinGecko API to fetch real-time Bitcoin exchange rates. Please refer to CoinGecko's Privacy Policy for information about their data practices.

5. Data Storage

All user data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage API. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers, except for API calls to CoinGecko for exchange rates.

6. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Enable or disable the extension at any time
  • Clear your tracked price history
  • Export your data
  • Change display preferences

7. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension's interface.
